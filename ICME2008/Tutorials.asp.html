Tutorials will take place on Monday, June 23, 2008. The cost for half-day tutorials are €50 for students and €100 for non-students. The cost for the full-day tutorial is €100 for students and €200 for non-students.

Monday Morning, June 23, 09:30 - 12:30

T-1: Distributed Video Coding for Low Cost Multimedia Communications and Systems
Presenter: W.A.C. Fernando, University of Surrey

T-2: Analysis and Retrieval Techniques for Motion and Music Data
Presenter: Meinard Müller, Max-Planck-Institut für Informatik

T-3: Learning Human Perception and Semantics Understanding in Multimedia Applications
Presenters: Cha Zhang, Microsoft Research and Tsuhan Chen, Carnegie Mellon University

Monday Afternoon, June 23, 14:30 - 17:30

T-4: Locality aware P2P-CDN: The way to scale Internet Video to the World
Presenter: Jin Li, Microsoft Research

T-5: Compression for 3DTV and FTV
Presenter: Aljoscha Smolic, Fraunhofer HHI

T-6: Semantic Indexing and Retrieval of Video
Presenter: Marcel Worring and Cees Snoek, University of Amsterdam

Monday, All day, June 23, 09:30 - 17:30

T-7: An Introduction to Bayesian Machine Learning for Multimedia Information Processing
Presenter: A. Taylan Cemgil, University of Cambridge
