ICME 2014 Registration
Welcome to ICME 2014!

Welcome to ICME 2014 at the 5-star Jinjiang Hotel in the city center of Chengdu, Sichuan, China on July 14-18, 2014! Chengdu is the Capital of Sichuan Province which is often referred to the Land of Abundance. Chengdu in Chinese also means the Capital of Success. Chengdu is not only rich in gourmet food but also rich in gorgeous scenic views, historical sites and culture heritages.

In ICME 2014, we have prepared a rich and polished technical program that includes prestigious keynote speeches, outstanding industry forums, excellent technical programs with 214 main conference papers, diverse yet dynamic workshops with 157 workshop papers, timely grand challenges, inspiring Ph.D. forums, exciting exhibitions and demos, and seminal yet free tutorials! We will also host an inclusive welcome reception that welcomes everyone who has registered for the conference or workshops. Moreover, a grand banquet and award ceremony night with wonderful performance and delicious food that blends in the unique Sichuan culture element is also inviting you to enjoy!

This exciting event sincerely welcomes everyone in the field to join with hundreds of experts and researchers from all around the world to foster conversation and collaboration. We hope you will enjoy the technical programs as well the nature beauties, culture heritages and gourmet food in Chengdu. We wish Chengdu and ICME 2014 will become your new starting point for a higher success in your career and life!

We look forward to seeing you all in Chengdu in July!

ICME 2014 Registration Policy

Please read through the ENTIRE ICME 2014 Registration Policy before starting the ICME 2014 registration as different registrations have different rules and benefits.

Anyone who wishes to attend any event of ICME 2014, including workshops, tutorials, main conference technical sessions, panel discussions, industry forums, keynote and plenary sessions, and exhibitions must register to attend. There are three types of registrations available for the attendees.

1. Conference Registration: This is the recommended and default registration type. This type of registration allows the registrant to attend all events of ICME 2014 on July 14-18, 2014, including all workshops, tutorials, main conference technical sessions, panel discussions, industry forums, keynote and plenary sessions, exhibitions, tea breaks during the whole conference, welcome reception and one electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings. Non-student conference registration also includes the conference grand banquet.

2. Exhibitor Registration: This type of registration is available for people who have submitted exhibitions or authors who would like to demonstrate their work at ICME 2014. This type of registration includes an exhibition table during the main conference and all the benefits of a non-student conference registration.

3. Workshop-Only Registration: This type of registration is available for people who wish to only attend the two-day workshop sessions held on July 14 and July 18, 2014, respectively. This type of registration includes all the workshops, tutorials, tea breaks during the workshops, welcome reception, and one electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings. However, registrants of this type are NOT allowed to attend any other events in the main conference.

Paper Registration for Main Conference/Workshop/Demo/Exhibition/Grand Challenge 

1. Author Registration Policy. Each accepted main conference paper, workshop paper, demo paper, or grand challenge paper, must have at least one author registered according to the following policy at a non-student and non-life-member rate by Saturday, May 31, 2014, in order for the paper to be included in the electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings distributed at the conference, and in the online conference or workshop proceedings in IEEE Xplorer database.
  • Each non-student and non-life-member Conference Registration can cover up to TWO papers of any kind (i.e., main conference papers, workshop papers, demo papers, or grand challenge papers) from the same author. For example, one such Conference Registration can cover: 2 main conference papers; or 1 main conference paper and 1 demo paper; or 2 workshop papers; or 1 grand challenge paper and 1 workshop paper; etc. 
  • Each non-student and non-life-member Workshop-Only Registration can cover only ONE workshop paper from the same author. 
  • Each Exhibitor Registration can cover only ONE paper of any kind (i.e., main conference paper, workshop paper, demo paper, or grand challenge paper) from the same author. 
2. Exhibition Registration Policy. Each accepted exhibition must have one person registered for the conference at the exhibitor rate. In case a regular paper author would like to demonstrate his or her work at an exhibition table during the main conference, he or she needs to register at the exhibitor rate and notify the registration chair.

3. Author No Show Policy. One of the co-authors (or a qualified substitute) of an accepted paper/demo must report to the session chair before the respective session starts according to the schedule published, and present the paper/demo in person at the conference/workshops site. Any paper accepted into the technical program, but not presented on-site will be automatically withdrawn from the conference/workshops and the proceedings. IEEE further reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g. removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference. 

4. If you have any questions regarding registration, please contact icme14@gmail.com.

Cancellation/Refund/Transfer Policy

General Attendees 
Cancellations/refund request should be made in writing to icme14@gmail.com. Cancellations will be subject to a US$105 cancellation fee before June 14, 2014. No Refunds will be made after JUNE 14, 2014.

Author Refund
At least one author of an accepted paper must register at a non-student and non-life-member rate per the paper registration policy above in order for paper to be included in the conference and workshop proceedings. If complete payment is not received by the author registration deadline May 31, 2014, the paper will be pulled from the publications in ICME 2014. Author registration is Non-Refundable.

Registration Transfer
Author Registration can be only transferred to another co-author under special circumstances. Registration of general attendees can only be transferred to another person with written notice. All transfer requests must be made in writing to icme14@gmail.com.

Registration package can only be picked up on site by the registrant in person, or by an authorized person specified in writing to icme14@gmail.com.

Visa Invitation Letter
Visa Invitation Letter will be only issued to registrant whose registration fee has been paid in full. It is the registrant’s own responsibility to check with his/her local Chinese Embassy or Consulate for all the required material to obtain a visa to China for ICME 2014. No Refunds will be made after June 14, 2014 even if you cannot obtain a visa to enter China. After your registration, please fill out the visa invitation letter request form and send it to icme14@gmail.com. Please make sure you have included your registration confirmation number (or registration id number) (or identify yourself if you are a keynote speaker; panelist; OC member for workshop or main conference; delegate of a sponsor; member of the 4 sponsoring TCs; ICME steering committee member; tutorial speaker; session chair; T-MM editorial board member; or in other role that doesn't require a registration to attend side meetings or conference activities - confirmed with registration chair) in the visa invitation letter request form.
