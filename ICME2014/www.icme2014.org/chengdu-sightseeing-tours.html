Chengdu Sightseeing Tours

1. ANCIENT SHU CULTURE TOUR
The ancient Shu culture is an ancient civilization originated in Sichuan including Sichuan Province, Chongqing, and other regions from ancient times to the early Spring and Autumn Period. Though with its own distinction, Shu culture is closely related to the Central Plain civilization. 
                                                    
Morning: Dufu’s Thatched Cottage

Dufu was a great Tang Dynasty poet. He lived in this house when he traveled to Chengdu. Dufu’s Thatched Cottage is the largest, best preserved, most unique, and best known residence of Du Fu.



Afternoon: Jinsha Site Museum-Sichuan Museum

Covering approximately 35,000 square meters of floor space, the Jinsha Site Museum
is a theme park set up for the protection, research, and exhibition of the Jinsha site and unearthed cultural relics. The Museum has a rich collection of precious gold, jade and ancient ivory art crafts rarely seen in other domestic cultural relics collections.


2. PANDA HABITAT WORLD CULTURAL HERITAGE + SANXINGDUI TOUR

Morning: Panda Habitat World

Be an early bird to see the most of these adorable pandas. The perfect time for visiting pandas would be 9:30am because pandas are semi-nocturnal and sleep in the middle of the day. Other recommended panda habitats: Bifeng Valley Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding and Wolong Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding.


Afternoon: Sanxingdui Museum

The Sanxingdui Museum covers around 12 sq km and its collection dates back 3,000 to 5,000 years. With the longest time span, it is the biggest and the most culturally rich archaeological site of an ancient city, state and culture. The best-preserved are the eastern, western and southern city walls and the city walls around Moon Bay. The site is known as one of the greatest archaeological discoveries of the 20th century. Being the source of Yangzte River Civilization signifies that the Yangtze River, along with the Yellow River, is also an essential part of the Chinese civilization.


3. SICHUAN CUISINE TOUR

Morning: Taste in Pi County

When it comes to Sichuan delicacies, bean paste is something you cannot miss out! In Pi County, the home of bean paste, there is a Sichuan Cuisine Museum, which is fantastic for lovers of fine food and dining. Here you will get a true understanding of Sichuan cuisine. Pi County is known as the Soul of Sichuan Cuisine, which is famous for the classic Sichuan dishes such as twice cooked pork, mapo tofu, fish with spicy bean paste and Sichuan hotpot. You must try all of them!


Afternoon: Jinli -- Paradise for Gourmets 

Jinli abounds with buildings of old times, delicious food, and local customs. Along the street you can find delicious snacks from food stalls and open-air cooking places. Just pick a table lined along both sides of the street, sit down and enjoy the local tastes, the famous sandapao (sticky rice), roast beef pastry, and yellow fermented glutinous rice etc. Once you taste it, you will surely go back for more! This street is definitely a must-go for foreign visitors. 


4. DUJIANGYAN + CHINA LANE TOUR

Morning: Dujiangyan

Summer is the best season for Dujiangyan. During summer, the Beer Festival is a particular attraction for its merry carousals. The Erwang Temple Fair and Dujiang Dam Water Festival are also a must-go for those who are interested in local customs and the Dujiangyan water culture. 
After visiting the 3,000-year-old Dujiangyan Water Works, you can treat yourself a good lunch of local cuisine at the downtown area. Any roadside eatery will make you just want to immerse yourself in this local culinary delights. 

Afternoon: China Lane (Wide and Narrow Alleys)

Lane configuration is a typical traditional housing style in Chengdu. With a total length of about 500 meters, this area consists of three parallel lanes, one wide, one narrow and one hatch-shaped (#), along which you will find courtyard compounds. The Wide Lane is for leisure while the Narrow Lane is for laid-back living, where you will find world-class retailers and fashion outlets. In the Jingxiangzi or the hatch-shaped lane are attractions for young people, particularly the trendy bars.
