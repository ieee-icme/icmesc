Call For Special Session Proposals

Special Session Paper Submission Instructions:

The EDAS system has  tracks for each special session. The special session authors please follow the guidelines at the ICME’07

website “For Authors”, and select the track corresponding to the desired special session. The page count for the special session papers

is according to the general submission guidelines for ICME’07.

 

The special session submission and review process is carried out as follows:

　

1.  Paper submission deadline for all special sessions: January 29, 2007.

2.  Notification of acceptance: March19, 2007.

3.  Camera ready paper due:   April 16,  2007.
