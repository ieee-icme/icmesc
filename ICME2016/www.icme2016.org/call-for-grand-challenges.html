Call for Grand Challenges

 
IEEE ICME 2016 Grand Challenges is an industry-sponsored program at the IEEE ICME 2016 Conference comprising multimedia-related contests. Each contest is organized by one or more industry sponsors.

The contests are intended to be challenging as well as entertaining, and may provide (cash) awards. Both individuals and teams are welcome to participate in the contests. Winners of each contest will be invited to participate in a final Grand Challenge presentation and award ceremony at IEEE ICME 2016.

IEEE ICME 2016 is inviting proposals for its Grand Challenges program. To submit a proposal for a Grand Challenge, please complete the form below, and submit it to the IEEE ICME 2016 grand challenges chairs. Proposals can be submitted at any time but no later than November 30, 2015, and they will be evaluated as they come.

Proposals should include a one page call for participation, including a Grand Challenge description, evaluation criteria, guidelines, and submission deadline. Challenges may be either soft (i.e., producing an output judged by humans) or hard (where the output is compared to ground truth). Examples of possible Grand Challenges:
  • Estimate the geo-coordinates for each of a test set of images.
  • Produce a panoramic image for a set of tourist photos of a place.
  • Image retrieval: Produce an algorithm for rank ordering image search results.
  • Social media prediction: Predict which images from a set, if posted to social media, will gather more “likes”.
For further information please contact the grand challenges chairs Andrew C. Gallagher (agallagher@google.com) and Christian Timmerer (christian.timmerer@itec.aau.at).
