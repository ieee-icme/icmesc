Call for Presentation of T-MM Papers


In order to better serve the multimedia community, IEEE Transactions on Multimedia (T-MM) is collaborating with IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo (ICME) to promote papers published in T-MM. Authors of T-MM papers will be given the opportunity to present their work at ICME 2016, which will be held in Seattle from July 11-15, 2016. The presentation will be subject to space availability and approval by the ICME Technical Program Chairs. T-MM papers published on or after January 1, 2015 and manuscripts accepted on or before April 15, 2016 are eligible for presentation at ICME 2016. Additionally, the T-MM paper should not be an extended version of a previous ICME paper.

Since the T-MM papers have already been peer-reviewed, they will not be subject to further review. They will be reprinted in the ICME proceedings, but will not appear redundantly in Xplore.
ICME 2016 aims to have high quality oral and poster presentations. Several awards sponsored by industry and institutions will be given out. Approval notifications will be issued no later than May 13, 2016 and all approved requests for presentation must have one author/presenter register for the conference according to the ICME 2016 registration policy.
