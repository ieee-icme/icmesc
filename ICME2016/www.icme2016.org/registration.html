ICME 2016 Registration Policy

Please read through the ENTIRE ICME 2016 Registration Policy before starting the ICME 2016 registration as different registrations have different rules and benefits.

Anyone who wishes to attend any event of ICME 2016, including workshops, tutorials, main conference technical sessions, panel discussions, industry forums, keynote and plenary sessions, and exhibitions must register to attend. There are two types of registrations available for the attendees.

1. Conference Registration: This is the recommended and default registration type. This type of registration allows the registrant to attend all events of ICME 2016 on July 11-15, 2016, including all workshops, tutorials, main conference technical sessions, panel discussions, industry forums, keynote and plenary sessions, exhibitions, tea breaks during the whole conference, and welcome reception, and includes one electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings. Non-student conference registration also includes the conference grand Banquet.

2. Workshop-Only Registration: This type of registration is available for people who wish to attend only the workshop sessions held on July 11 and July 15, 2016, respectively. This type of registration includes all the workshops, tutorials, tea breaks during the workshops, welcome reception, and one electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings. However, registrants of this type are NOT allowed to attend any other events in the main conference.

Paper Registration for Main Conference/Workshop/Demo/Grand Challenge

1. Author Registration Policy. Each accepted main conference paper, workshop paper, demo paper, industry track paper, or grand challenge paper, must have at least one author registered according to the following policy at a non-student and non-life-member rate by Sunday, May 15, 2016, in order for the paper to be included in the electronic copy of conference and workshop proceedings distributed at the conference, and in the online conference or workshop proceedings in IEEE Xplorer database.

  • Each non-student and non-life-member Conference Registration can cover up to TWO papers of any kind (i.e., main conference papers, workshop papers, demo papers, industry track papers, grand challenge papers, or T-MM papers presentations) from the same author. For example, one such Conference Registration can cover: 2 main conference papers; or 1 main conference paper and 1 demo paper; or 2 workshop papers; or 1 grand challenge paper and 1 workshop paper; etc. Note that a demo paper associated with a regular paper from the same author will be considered to be already covered by the corresponding accepted paper.
  • Each non-student and non-life-member Workshop-Only Registration can cover only ONE workshop paper from the same author.

2. Author No Show Policy. One of the co-authors of an accepted paper/demo must report to the session chair before the respective session starts according to the schedule published, and present the paper/demo in person at the conference/workshops site. Under extraordinary unplanned circumstances, a qualified substitute may present on the authors’ behalf, with the technical program chairs’ permission. Any paper accepted into the technical program, but not presented on-site will be automatically withdrawn from the conference/workshops and the proceedings. IEEE further reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (e.g. removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference.

3. If you have any questions regarding registration, please contact sp-conf-icme2016-reg@ieee.org.

Cancellation/Refund/Transfer Policy

General Attendees

Cancellations/refund request should be made in writing to sp-conf-icme2016-reg@ieee.org. Cancellations will be subject to a US$105 cancellation fee before June 11, 2016. No Refunds will be made after JUNE 11, 2016.

Author Refund

At least one author of an accepted paper must register at a non-student and non-life-member rate per the paper registration policy above in order for paper to be included in the conference and workshop proceedings. If complete payment is not received by the author registration deadline May 15, 2016, the paper will be pulled out from the publications in ICME 2016. Author registration is Non-Refundable.

Registration Transfer

Author Registration can be only transferred to another co-author under special circumstances. Registration of general attendees can only be transferred to another person with written notice. All transfer requests must be made in writing to sp-conf-icme2016-reg@ieee.org. Registration transfer will be subject to a US$50 transfer fee.

Registration package can only be picked up on site by the registrant in person, or by an authorized person specified in writing to sp-conf-icme2016-reg@ieee.org.

Visa Invitation Letter

Visa Invitation Letters will be issued only to registrants whose registration fee has been paid in full. It is the registrant’s own responsibility to check with his/her local USA Embassy or Consulate for all the required material to obtain a visa for ICME 2016. No Refunds will be made after June 11, 2016 even if you cannot obtain a visa to enter USA. After your registration, please fill out the visa invitation letter request form and send it to sp-conf-icme2016-reg@ieee.org. Please make sure you have included your registration confirmation number (or registration id number) in the visa invitation letter request form.
