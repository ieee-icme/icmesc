3MT Competition @ Grand III

The 3MT is an interesting yet challenging way to present a graduate research thesis to a general audience in just three minutes using a SINGLE PowerPoint slide! Our 3MT competition will closely follow the recommended rules and judging criteria listed at http://threeminutethesis.org/rules-judging-criteria. Participating students should prepare their presentations according to the rules and criteria.

The 3MT awards committee will consist of 3 judges (who will cast scores) and one moderator (Student Program Co-Chair Ivan Bajic, who will not vote). For each presentation in the 3MT competition, a 3-minute slot (sharp) is allocated for the presentation and 1-minute for changeover to the next presenter. Awards will be given to the best 3MT presentations.

Student-Mentor Luncheon @ Fifth Avenue

The Student-Mentor Luncheon provides a venue for students to meet and interact with industry partners in a relaxed and informal setting and explore future collaboration or possible career prospects. Participating students will be assigned mentors, and the student-mentor pairs will sit together during the first part of the luncheon at the same table to discuss student’s research and other topics of common interest. An ID tag will be provided to each student to be placed on the table where they sit, in order to facilitate meeting with the mentor. In the latter part, the luncheon event will also have a 30-minute time-slot for free-chat between the students and other attendees. In the luncheon, the students and industry members will be on hand to discuss a variety of topics including but not limited to:

  • Students’ specific research directions and interesting research results
  • Current industry projects and major technical trends
  • Internships and career opportunities
  • Career advice for graduating students
  • Important skills sets for being successful in industry
  • Technology transfer and start-up opportunities
Students shall feel free to bring some slides/materials to talk to the mentors. Industry participants include Amazon, Cisco, MERL, Microsoft, Ricoh, and other companies.
