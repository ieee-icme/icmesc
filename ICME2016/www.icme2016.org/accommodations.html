Accommodations


Recently renovated, the Westin Seattle is a four star downtown Seattle hotel. It has 891 smoke-free guest rooms and suites featuring panoramic views of the whole area. It is located in Seattle's financial and shopping district and is within walking distance many attractions.

Summer is a great season for Seattle, and hotel rooms at competitive prices are hard to find. The ICME 2016 local team has worked very hard to secure a very favorable room rate in the Westin Seattle. The group rate for a single occupancy room is $249 per night. For early bird, there is a further $40 discount for a limited amount of rooms, bringing the room rate to $209. To take advantage of the early bird rate, please book your room as soon as possible here.

For your convenience, we strongly recommend you to book with the main venue hotel. We have negotiated a significant discount with Westin Hotel, and the availability is limited. So, please make your reservation at your earliest convenience.




Alternative hotels:

Hotel room availability and price may fluctuate by day with travel adjustments and cancellations. You are also welcome to browse online and make your own choice.
