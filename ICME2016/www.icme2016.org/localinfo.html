Local Transportation

Walk: Seattle has a very walkable downtown, with nearby attractions (several shown on the map below) within 20 minutes' walk of the Westin.

Bus: We recommend using public transportation in the Seattle Metro area. The trip planner is a very useful tool for figuring out which buses to take. Just enter the starting and ending points, the website will plan a bus trip for you.

Uber: It is very popular in the Seattle metro area; you can usually get a ride in a few minutes.

Cab: One can also call one of the following companies for cabs:
  • Orange Cab Company: 206-444-0409
  • Seattle Yellow Cab: 206-622-6500
  • Seattle Union Cab: 206-307-9716
Monorail: The Seattle Monorail, like the Space Needle built for the 1962 Seattle World's Fair, is a convenient way to travel from Westlake Center (across the street from the Westin) to Seattle Center. See our transportation to our Reception here for more information.
