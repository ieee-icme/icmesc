Transportation

Bus: We strongly recommend to use public transportations in the Seattle Metro area. Metro planner is a very useful tool for figuring out which buses to take. Just enter the starting and ending points, the website will plan a bus trip for you.

Uber: It is very popular in Seattle metro area, you can usually get a ride in a few minutes.

Cab: One can also call one of the following companies for cabs:
  • Orange Cab Company: 206-444-0409
  • Seattle Yellow Cab: 206-622-6500
  • Seattle Union Cab: 206-307-9716
