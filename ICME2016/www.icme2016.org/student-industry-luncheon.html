Student-Industry Luncheon


Thursday, July 14, 2016

The Student-Industry Luncheon is reserved for those students who have applied to participate before the deadline.

Student-Industry Luncheon @ Fifth Avenue

The luncheon provides a venue provides a venue for students to meet and interact with industry partners in a relaxed and informal setting and explore future collaboration or possible career prospects. Participating students will be assigned mentors, and the student-mentor pairs will sit together during the first part of the luncheon at the same table to discuss student’s research and other topics of common interest. An ID tag will be provided to each student to be placed on the table where they sit, in order to facilitate meeting with the mentor. In the latter part, the luncheon event will also have a 30-minute time-slot for free-chat between the students and other attendees. In the luncheon, the students and industry members will be on hand to discuss a variety of topics including but not limited to:

  • Students’ specific research directions and interesting research results
  • Current industry projects and major technical trends
  • Internships and career opportunities
  • Career advice for graduating students
  • Important skills sets for being successful in industry
  • Technology transfer and start-up opportunities
Students shall feel free to bring some slides/materials to talk to the mentors. Industry participants include Amazon, Cisco, MERL, Microsoft, Ricoh, and other companies.
