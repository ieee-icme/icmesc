Call for tutorials

ICME05 provides tutorials (either half-day (three hours) or full-day (six hours)). Appropriate subjects for tutorials include those in the area of multimedia processing, applications, standards, and systems. These tutorials should be addressed to people with little background in these subjects and/or should cover new and advanced topics aiming at experts in the field. All tutorial proposals should be submitted to Theo Gevers.

Prospective organizers of tutorial should submit proposals indicating the following:

  • Title of the tutorial
  • Half-day or full-day
  • An abstract of less than 200 words, including a motivation of the need for the tutorial at ICME05
  • An outline of the tutorial
  • Intended audience
  • Biographies of the proposers

Proposals will be evaluated based on the timeliness of the topic, qualifications of the organizers and lectures, and balance of topics across technical areas within ICME05.

 Deadlines

  • January 18, 2005 (10 am CET): Submission of proposals
  • March 1, 2005: Notification of acceptance
  • July 5, 2005: Tutorial
