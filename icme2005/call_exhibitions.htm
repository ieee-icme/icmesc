Call for exhibitions

Goal

The exhibition aims at an academic exchange of knowledge and ideas between researchers triggered by appealing demonstrations of multimedia technology.

Proposals

Proposals for the exhibition are due January 15, 2005. Proposals are free-format but should address a description of the topic, the relation to scientific research, the exhibition format, and the technical requirements.

The exhibition committee (chair and co-chairs) will make a selection based on quality (innovation and attractivity). All the proposals should be submitted by email to Jurgen den Hartog or to Jan Nesvadba. Upon acception, each selected demonstration will be entitled to have maximum 2 pages in the final proceedings and the CD of the conference. See the submission page for more details.

Date and cost

Demostrations of multimedia technology take place on July 7, the second day of the conference (cost 500 Euro per table). A booth selling books can be set up for the full duration of the conference (cost 2100 Euro per booth).

 Deadlines

  • January 18, 2005 (10 am CET): submission of proposals
  • February 1, 2005: notification of acceptance
  • April 30, 2005: deadline for submission of camera-ready full versions of the papers
  • July 7, 2005: demonstration day
