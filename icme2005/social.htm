Social Events

  

Reception (Wednesday, 6 July)

On Wednesday July 6, a reception in the Town Hall is offered to the delegates of the conference by the Major and Aldermen of the city of Amsterdam.

 

Canal boats will be waiting for everybody at 18:00h in the canal behind the conference venue (Krasnapolsky) to take them on a special boat tour to show the beautiful Amsterdam. The boats will bring everybody to the Town Hall where the reception takes place from 19:00-20:00h. The address of the Town Hall is Amstel 1.

 

 

Conference dinner (Thursday, 7 July)

The conference dinner will be held at one of the most reputable buildings in the city centre of Amsterdam, the West India House. Canal boats will be waiting for you behind the Krasnapolsky hotel and will leave at 18:30h to the West India House. The dinner will be starting at 19:30h.

 

If you have registered for the conference dinner you will receive your banquet ticket upon registration at the conference desk. The address of the West India House is Herenmarkt 99.
