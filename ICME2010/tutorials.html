Call for Tutorials

The ICME 2010 organizing committee invites proposals for tutorials, which are to take place on the first day of the conference, July 19, 2010.

Proposal Guidelines

Each tutorial proposal must include a title, a description (no more than 3 pages) stating clear motivation/justification for the topic to be presented at ICME, a comprehensive outline of the proposed content, and the duration (half day or full day) of the proposed tutorial. A half-page biosketch of each tutorial presenter must also be included.

Submission

Tutorial proposals must be in PDF format and submitted via email to the Tutorial Chairs: Ton Kalker (ton.kalker@ieee.org) and Weisi Lin (wslin@ntu.edu.sg).

Important Dates

Tutorial Proposal Due: January 31, 2010
Notification of Acceptance: February 28, 2010
