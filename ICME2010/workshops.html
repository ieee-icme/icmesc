Call for Workshop Proposals

Workshop proposals are solicited for ICME 2010. ICME workshops should facilitate active discussions and constructive interactions on current or emerging topics of interest. They should allow members of the community to compare and discuss approaches, methods, and new concepts on research topics pertinent to the main conference.

Workshop proposals for ICME 2010 should include:

  • The title, goals and duration of the workshop (half-day or full-day);
  • The specific topics that will be addressed by the workshop;
  • The relevance and significance of this workshop to the main conference;
  • A draft workshop call for papers with:
    • The names, affiliations, addresses, phone numbers and email addresses of the workshop organizers, who should be experts in the related topics and preferably from multiple institutions;
    • The paper selection process;
    • A list of potential Program Committee members and their affiliations.

The proposals should not exceed 3 pages and should be submitted by December 31, 2009 to the Workshop Chairs, Alexander Loui (alexander.loui@kodak.com) and Ishfaq Ahmad (iahmad@cse.uta.edu). Each proposal will be evaluated based on various criteria including the quality and level of details of the proposal, the theme of the workshop, its relevance to the main conference, and the overall balance of the conference workshop program.
